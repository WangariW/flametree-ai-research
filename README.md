# FlameTree AI Research

## Overview

This repository documents my research and analysis of FlameTree AI, a conversational AI platform designed to improve customer engagement through intelligent AI-powered agents.

The research explores how conversational AI, Large Language Models (LLMs), and AI agents can be applied to enterprise environments to automate customer interactions, improve operational efficiency, and support business growth. Particular attention is given to applications within the Banking and Financial Services (BFSI) sector.

---

## Research Objectives

The primary objectives of this research were to:

* Understand how conversational AI platforms operate in enterprise environments.
* Explore the role of AI agents in customer engagement and business automation.
* Investigate practical applications of AI within banking and financial services.
* Analyze how Large Language Models can support customer support and knowledge management systems.
* Develop a deeper understanding of enterprise AI adoption and implementation challenges.

---
