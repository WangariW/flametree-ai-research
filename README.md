# FlameTree AI Research Repository

This repository contains my research and analysis on FlameTree AI, a conversational AI platform designed to improve customer engagement through intelligent AI agents.

## Overview

FlameTree AI focuses on enabling organizations to build AI-driven conversational systems capable of handling customer interactions across multiple channels. The platform is particularly relevant to enterprise environments such as banking and financial services.

## Topics Covered

* Conversational AI systems
* AI agents and orchestration
* Enterprise AI applications in BFSI
* Multichannel customer engagement systems
* Use of LLMs in real-world business workflows

## Key Learning Areas

* Understanding how AI agents simulate human-like customer interactions
* Role of context-aware AI in improving customer service
* Banking use cases for AI automation
* Challenges in deploying AI in regulated environments
