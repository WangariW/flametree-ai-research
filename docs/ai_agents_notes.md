# AI Agents Notes

## What is an AI Agent?

An AI agent is a software system capable of perceiving information, making decisions, and performing actions to achieve a specific objective.

Unlike traditional chatbots that simply respond to user inputs, AI agents can maintain context, use external tools, retrieve information, and make decisions based on goals and available data.

## Characteristics of AI Agents

### Context Awareness

Agents maintain awareness of conversation history and relevant information.

### Goal-Oriented Behavior

Agents are designed to achieve specific objectives rather than simply answering questions.

### Decision Making

Agents can evaluate available information and determine appropriate actions.

### Tool Usage

Modern AI agents can interact with databases, APIs, documents, and external systems.

## Applications of AI Agents

### Customer Support

Handling routine customer inquiries and support requests.

### Banking

Assisting customers with account information, loan inquiries, and product recommendations.

### Knowledge Management

Providing employees with access to organizational knowledge and documentation.

### Business Automation

Supporting repetitive workflows and operational processes.

## Relevance to Enterprise AI

Organizations increasingly use AI agents to improve productivity, reduce costs, and enhance customer experience.

AI agents represent an important step toward intelligent automation and are expected to play a significant role in future enterprise software systems.
